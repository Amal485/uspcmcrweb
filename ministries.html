Ministry

Worship Ministry

At USPC Manchester, worship is not just a Sunday activity — it's a lifestyle. Our worship team is passionate about leading the congregation into an authentic encounter with God through Spirit-filled praise and worship.

We believe that worship is the gateway into God's presence, and we take seriously the call to prepare and present offerings of worship that honour Him.

  • Live worship every Sunday morning and evening
  • Regular worship nights and soaking sessions
  • Opportunities to join the choir and praise team
  • Instrument and vocal training for members

Ministry

Youth & Children

We believe deeply in investing in the next generation. Our children's and youth ministries provide age-appropriate, engaging, and Spirit-filled environments where young people can encounter God for themselves.

From Sunday School for the youngest members to a dedicated youth programme for teenagers, we are committed to raising a generation who know who they are in Christ.

  • Sunday School — every Sunday 9:30–10:15 AM
  • Kids Club during main service
  • Weekly youth meetings and discipleship
  • Youth camps, trips, and social events
  • Vacation Bible School (annual)

Ministry

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry is a community of women who are passionate about their faith, supporting one another, and growing together in Christ. Through regular meetings, conferences, and discipleship, we help women discover and walk in their God-given purpose.

  • Monthly women's meetings and Bible studies
  • Annual women's conferences and retreats
  • Mentoring and discipleship programmes
  • Prayer groups and support networks

Ministry

Men's Ministry

Our Men's Ministry is committed to raising up godly men — in the home, in the church, and in the community. We provide a space where men can be honest, grow in faith, and hold one another accountable.

  • Regular men's fellowships and Bible studies
  • Annual men's conference and retreats
  • Accountability groups and mentoring
  • Community service and outreach projects

Ministry

Community Outreach

Jesus commanded us to be salt and light in the world. Our community outreach ministry takes the love of God outside our walls — serving Manchester with compassion, meeting practical needs, and sharing the good news of the Gospel.

  • Food bank and community meals
  • Street evangelism and door-to-door outreach
  • Visiting the sick, elderly, and isolated
  • Supporting local charities and initiatives
  • Prayer for Manchester and its neighbourhoods
