Who We Are

A Community Built on Faith, Love & Unity

United Shalom Pentecostal Church Manchester is a Spirit-filled, Bible-believing church dedicated to sharing the love of God with our community and beyond. We are part of the United Shalom Pentecostal Church family and are committed to making disciples of all nations.

Whether you are new to faith, returning, or simply searching — our doors are open to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the transforming power of God's love, and we strive to be a church where all are welcomed and none are forgotten.
