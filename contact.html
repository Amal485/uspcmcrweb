Frequently Asked Questions

Planning Your First Visit?

Here are some answers to common questions about visiting USPC Manchester.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! There is no dress code at USPC Manchester. We want you to feel comfortable and welcome from the moment you arrive.

How long is the Sunday service?

Sunday School runs 9:30–10:00 AM. The English Service follows from 10:15–11:15 AM, then the Malayalam Service from 11:15 AM–1:15 PM. Feel free to join for any or all of these.

Is there childcare available?

Yes! We have a dedicated children's programme running during the Sunday service, so children are well cared for while parents worship.

Do I need to be a Christian to attend?

Not at all. Everyone is welcome, regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey. Come as you are — we'd love to meet you.

Is parking available?

We are located at 89 Floatshall Rd, Wythenshawe, Manchester M23 1JB. Street parking is available nearby. Use the map on this page for directions.

How can I get involved?

We'd love for you to get involved! Whether it's worship, youth, outreach, or prayer — there's a ministry for everyone. Speak to us after service or contact us online.
